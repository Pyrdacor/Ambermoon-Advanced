AS YOU APPROACH THE UNDEAD HE BLICKS UP TO YOU AND HIS GLISTENING BLUE EYES STARE AT YOU. THEN A BLOODCURDLING SCREAM MOVES THROUGH THE AIR OF THE CAVE. TOO LATE YOU NOTICE THE SPELLBINDING CIRCLE ON THE GROUND YOU HAVE JUST ENTERED. THE UNDEAD WARRIOR RAISES HIS SWORD AND SUMMONES SOME UNDEAD MINIONS. THEN HE SCREAMS AGAIN AND PREPARES FOR ATTACK.
