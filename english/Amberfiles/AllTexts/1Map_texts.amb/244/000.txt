AS SOON AS YOU REACH THE COORDINATES FROM TORLE'S JOURNAL, A GIANT WHITE WHALE APPEARS. ITS BLOWHOLE SHOOTS AN ENORMOUS FOUNTAIN INTO THE AIR AND A TORRENT OF WATER FALLS DOWN ONTO YOUR DECK.^^ "I AM GROMBA, ONE OF THE FIVE SAGES OF THE SEA. I HAVE WATCHED YOU FOR QUITE A WHILE NOW AND AM SURE THAT YOU ARE WORTHY HEROS. MY FAVOUR IS YOURS. WHEN YOU HAVE CONVINCED US ALL, YOU SHOULD GO AND MEET THE OCEAN QUEEN. GOOD LUCK."
