"ALL HIS EFFORTS HAD ONCE AGAIN FAILED. FRUSTRATED, HE SAW ONLY ONE LAST HOPE OF ACHIEVING HIS GOAL. HE PLANNED TO HARNESS THE POWERS OF THE UNDEAD AND USE THEM TO CREATE THE GOLDEN STONE. SO HE FACED THE UNDEAD ARMY AND CHANTED SOME INCANTATIONS. AT FIRST, IT SEEMED TO WORK. THE UNDEAD SOULS FLEW TOWARD HIM TO UNITE THEIR STRENGTH. BUT THEY INVADED DIOKHAR'S BODY AND RIPPED HIM FROM LIFE."
