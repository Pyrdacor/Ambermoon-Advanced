ONCE THE DEMON IS DEFEATED TARGOR RETURNS TO HIS NORMAL FORM. BUT THE ENEMY ALSO TRANSFORMS INTO A MYSTIC.^^ "CONGRATULATIONS, TARGOR. YOU HAVE SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED THE TEST. AS A REWARD YOU MAY CARRY THE POWERFUL SPELL WITHIN YOU EVEN IF YOU LEAVE THIS TOWER. MAY IT SERVE YOU WELL."^^ "ONE MORE THING. FROM NOW ON, AS A WORTHY GRADUATE, YOU MAY USE OUR LIBRARY AND MYSTIC SUPPLY STORE. FAREWELL."^^ WITH THESE WORDS THE OLD MYSTIC RAISES HIS ARMS AND THE STONE HEAD VANISHES. ALL MEMBERS OF THE GROUP ALSO OPEN THEIR EYES AGAIN. SHORTLY AFTER THE MYSTIC LEAVES THE ROOM SATISFIED.
