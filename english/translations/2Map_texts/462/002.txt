AFTER YOU CLIMBED UP THE LADDED YOU NOTICE THAT THE WHOLE UPPER LEVEL IS IN RUINS. PLANTS OVERGROW THE REMAINING WALLS AND ROCKS AND YOU CAN SPOT SOME MORTAL REMAINS. BUT WHAT IS EVEN STRANGER IS THE FACT THAT THERE ARE BURNING CANDLES UP HERE AS IF SOMEONE OR SOMETHING STILL LIVES HERE. AN UNNATURAL COLD BREEZE BLOWS INTO YOUR FACES.
